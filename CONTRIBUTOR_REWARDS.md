# Contributor Rewards Program

We're building CodeLoop in the open and we want to hear from you. Every accepted bug report, feature request or thoughtful comment earns you **14 days of CodeLoop on us**.

## How the reward works

| You're on... | When your contribution is approved... |
|---|---|
| **Free trial** | We extend your trial by **14 days**. |
| **Paid plan** | We add **14 days of credit** to your next billing period. Multiple accepted contributions stack. |

Approval is at our team's discretion. Reports that are duplicates, spam or low-effort don't qualify. We aim to review every submission within **3 business days**.

---

## Two ways to submit

### 1. From your dashboard (recommended)

Sign in and visit **[codeloop.tech/dashboard/contribute](https://codeloop.tech/dashboard/contribute)**.

The structured form captures everything reviewers need: OS, IDE, app type, framework, repro steps, expected vs. actual behaviour, and a run ID. You can attach up to **10 files** (images, videos, logs) up to **200 MB total** — drag-and-drop with previews.

### 2. By email

Email **[contributions@mg.codeloop.tech](mailto:contributions@mg.codeloop.tech)** *from the address on your CodeLoop account*. Attachments are forwarded automatically. We try to extract environment info from the body, so it really helps to include lines like:

```
OS: macOS 14.5
IDE: Cursor 0.45
CodeLoop CLI: 0.1.12
App type: flutter
Run ID: artifacts/runs/abc-123

Steps to reproduce:
1. ...
2. ...

Expected: ...
Actual: ...
```

## What makes a great contribution

- A clear, specific **subject** — e.g. *"Video recording fails on Windows when the app is fullscreen"*.
- Your **OS, IDE, CodeLoop CLI / MCP server versions**.
- **Repro steps** a reviewer can follow in under 5 minutes.
- **Expected vs. actual** behaviour.
- **Attachments**: a screenshot or a 30-second screen recording almost always speeds up triage.
- The **run ID** from `artifacts/runs/<id>/` if the issue happened during a CodeLoop verification loop.

## What we count as "useful"

Anything that helps us make the product better:

- ✅ Reproducible bugs in any CodeLoop tool, runner, CLI, dashboard or website.
- ✅ Concrete feature requests (with a use case — not just "add X").
- ✅ Documentation gaps you noticed while onboarding.
- ✅ Onboarding friction reports — what didn't work first time.
- ✅ Performance regressions and stability issues.

## What doesn't qualify

- ❌ Duplicates of issues already reported.
- ❌ Auto-generated reports, AI-spamming the form, or content with no actionable detail.
- ❌ Questions ("how do I…?") — please use [support@codeloop.tech](mailto:support@codeloop.tech) instead.
- ❌ Security vulnerabilities — please disclose directly to [support@codeloop.tech](mailto:support@codeloop.tech).
- ❌ Reports about third-party products that aren't integrated with CodeLoop.

## Anti-abuse limits

To keep the program fair we apply:

- A maximum of **5 submissions per user per day**.
- Minimum lengths on the subject (10 chars) and body (50 chars).
- Standard spam filtering on inbound email (Mailgun spam flag).
- Audit trails on every submission and decision.

Spam, harassment or abuse will result in disqualification and account suspension.

---

## What happens after you submit

1. **Confirmation email** within seconds — your submission is queued.
2. **Admin review** within 3 business days. We may ask follow-up questions.
3. **Decision email** with a link back to your contribution page. If approved, your account is updated immediately:
   - Trial users: trial expiry pushed out by 14 days.
   - Paid users: +14 days banked, applied at the start of your next billing period.

You can always track status under **Your submissions** on the dashboard contribute page.
